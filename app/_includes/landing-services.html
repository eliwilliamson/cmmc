Quality

Total Quality Management is both a philosophy and a set of guiding principles that represent the foundation of a continuously improving organization at Cincinnati Mine Machinery Company. TQM is the application of quantitative methods and human resources to improve the material and services supplied to an organization, all the processes within an organization, and the degree to which the needs of the customer are met, now and in the future.
